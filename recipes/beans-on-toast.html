Beans on toast boiii

Description

This dish is hearty english food, perfect for lunch

Ingredients

  • Bread (2 slices)
  • Heinz Baked Beans
  • Butter
  • Grated Cheddar
  • HP Brown Sauce

Instructions

  1. First, add your baked beans to a sauce pan on a medium to high heat. Season, and bring to the boil before allowing to simmer for 5 mins.
  2. Whilst the beans are cooking add your bread to the toaster.
  3. When the bread is toasted, add to plate and spread butter.
  4. When cooked, add the beans on top of the toast. Apply brown sauce to taste.
  5. Add grated cheese on top.
  6. Enjoy!
