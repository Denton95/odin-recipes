Lemon and Garlic Chicken

Description

Breakfast, lunch, dessert, or snack time, any time's the right time for this delicious banana smoothie.

Ingredients

  • 2 bananas, broken into chunks
  • 2 cups milk
  • Â½ cup peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons honey, or to taste
  • 2 cups ice cubes
  • Anything you'd like to add! Think chocolate, whey protein, oats, seeds.

Instructions

  1. Place bananas, milk, peanut butter, honey, and ice cubes in a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth, about 30 seconds.
  3. Enjoy!
